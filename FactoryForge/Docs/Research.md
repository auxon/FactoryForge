 # FactoryForge Research Guide

## Overview

Research is the key to unlocking advanced buildings, recipes, and bonuses in FactoryForge. By researching technologies, you'll gain access to more efficient machines, better logistics, and powerful upgrades.

1. **Open the Research Menu**: Press the **lab icon** (‚öóÔ∏è) in the bottom toolbar (third button from the left)
2. **View Available Technologies**: Technologies are organized in a 3-tier grid
3. **Select Technology**: Tap on any available technology to start researching it

## How Research Works

### Science Packs
Research requires **science packs** - special items produced in labs that represent scientific knowledge:

- **Automation Science Pack (Red)**: Basic research pack, crafted from iron plates and copper cables
- **Logistic Science Pack (Green)**: Advanced research pack, requires automation science packs to unlock

### Research Process

1. **Choose a Technology**: Select any technology that meets the prerequisites
2. **Provide Science Packs**: The technology's cost in science packs is shown
3. **Fill Labs with Science Packs**: Put science packs into lab inventories for research to progress
4. **Wait for Completion**: Research takes time based on the technology's complexity
5. **Collect Results**: Once complete, the technology unlocks new recipes and bonuses

### Research Time
- Technologies take varying amounts of time to research
- Research speed can be increased with research speed bonuses
- You can only research one technology at a time

### Using Labs for Research

**Labs are the heart of your research operation!** Here's how to use them:

1. **Build Labs**: Craft and place lab buildings (requires automation science packs to unlock)
2. **Power Labs**: Labs need electricity to function - connect them to power poles
3. **Fill with Science Packs**: Double-tap on a lab to open its inventory, then transfer science packs from your inventory to the lab
4. **Monitor Progress**: Research progress is shown in the Research menu as a percentage
5. **Multiple Labs**: Build multiple labs and fill them all with science packs to speed up research

**Important**: Science packs must be in lab inventories, not just in your player inventory!

## Technology Tiers

### Tier 1: Basic Automation (Red Science Only)
These foundational technologies unlock core automation capabilities:

#### Automation
- **Cost**: 10 Red Science Packs
- **Time**: 10 seconds
- **Unlocks**: Assembling Machine 1
- **Benefit**: Automated crafting machines for efficient production

#### Logistics
- **Cost**: 20 Red Science Packs
- **Time**: 15 seconds
- **Unlocks**: Fast Inserter, Long-handed Inserter
- **Benefit**: Faster and longer-range item transfer

#### Turrets
- **Cost**: 10 Red Science Packs
- **Time**: 10 seconds
- **Unlocks**: Gun Turret
- **Benefit**: Defensive structures for base protection

#### Stone Walls
- **Cost**: 10 Red Science Packs
- **Time**: 10 seconds
- **Unlocks**: Stone Wall
- **Benefit**: Defensive walls to protect your base

#### Steel Processing
- **Cost**: 50 Red Science Packs
- **Time**: 20 seconds
- **Unlocks**: Steel Plate smelting
- **Benefit**: Access to steel, a key material for advanced machines

#### Military
- **Cost**: 20 Red Science Packs
- **Time**: 15 seconds
- **Unlocks**: Piercing Rounds Magazine
- **Benefit**: Better ammunition for combat

### Tier 2: Advanced Technologies (Red + Green Science)

#### Logistic Science Pack
- **Cost**: 75 Red Science Packs
- **Time**: 30 seconds
- **Prerequisites**: Automation
- **Unlocks**: Green science pack production
- **Benefit**: Enables advanced research technologies

#### Automation 2
- **Cost**: 40 Red + 40 Green Science Packs
- **Time**: 30 seconds
- **Prerequisites**: Automation, Logistic Science Pack
- **Unlocks**: Assembling Machine 2
- **Benefit**: Faster automated crafting

#### Logistics 2
- **Cost**: 40 Red + 40 Green Science Packs
- **Time**: 30 seconds
- **Prerequisites**: Logistics, Logistic Science Pack
- **Unlocks**: Fast Transport Belt
- **Benefit**: Faster item transport on belts

#### Advanced Material Processing
- **Cost**: 50 Red + 50 Green Science Packs
- **Time**: 30 seconds
- **Prerequisites**: Steel Processing
- **Unlocks**: Steel Furnace
- **Benefit**: Faster smelting using steel furnaces

#### Solar Energy
- **Cost**: 100 Red + 100 Green Science Packs
- **Time**: 60 seconds
- **Prerequisites**: Logistic Science Pack
- **Unlocks**: Solar Panel
- **Benefit**: Renewable energy generation

#### Electric Energy Accumulators
- **Cost**: 100 Red + 100 Green Science Packs
- **Time**: 60 seconds
- **Prerequisites**: Solar Energy
- **Unlocks**: Accumulator
- **Benefit**: Energy storage for solar power systems

#### Laser Turrets
- **Cost**: 150 Red + 150 Green Science Packs
- **Time**: 60 seconds
- **Prerequisites**: Turrets, Logistic Science Pack
- **Unlocks**: Laser Turret
- **Benefit**: Powerful defensive turrets

#### Mining Productivity 1
- **Cost**: 100 Red + 100 Green Science Packs
- **Time**: 60 seconds
- **Prerequisites**: Logistic Science Pack
- **Benefit**: 10% increase to mining output

#### Research Speed 1
- **Cost**: 100 Red + 100 Green Science Packs
- **Time**: 60 seconds
- **Prerequisites**: Logistic Science Pack
- **Benefit**: 20% increase to research speed

## Visual Indicators

### Technology States
- **Available**: Light blue background - can be researched now
- **Researching**: Yellow/gold background - currently being researched
- **Completed**: Green background - research finished, benefits active
- **Locked**: Gray background - prerequisites not met

### Research Progress
- **Research Info**: Shows current research name on the right side
- **Progress Bar**: Visual progress indicator at bottom
- **Percentage**: Shows completion percentage

### Button Colors
- **Completed**: Green tint for finished technologies
- **Researching**: Gold tint for active research
- **Available**: Light blue tint for accessible technologies
- **Unavailable**: Gray for locked technologies

## Research Strategy Tips

### Early Game Focus
1. **Prioritize Automation**: Research "Automation" first to unlock assembling machines
2. **Get Green Science**: Research "Logistic Science Pack" to unlock advanced technologies
3. **Improve Logistics**: Research "Logistics" and "Logistics 2" for better item transport

### Mid Game Goals
1. **Steel Production**: Research "Steel Processing" and "Advanced Material Processing"
2. **Better Machines**: Research "Automation 2" for faster crafting
3. **Defense**: Research "Turrets" and "Laser Turrets" for base protection

### Late Game Bonuses
1. **Mining Productivity**: Increases resource output
2. **Research Speed**: Makes future research faster
3. **Energy Systems**: Solar panels and accumulators for sustainable power

### Science Pack Production
- **Automation Science Packs**: Craft in labs using iron plates and copper cables
- **Logistic Science Packs**: Require inserters and transport belts
- **Production Scaling**: Build multiple labs and maintain steady science pack production

## Prerequisites System

Many technologies require other technologies to be researched first:
- Tier 2 technologies generally require "Logistic Science Pack"
- Advanced technologies often require their basic counterparts
- Some technologies form chains (e.g., Solar Energy ‚Üí Electric Energy Accumulators)

## Research Queue

FactoryForge currently supports researching one technology at a time. Future updates may add research queues for multiple simultaneous research projects.

## Tips for Success

1. **Maintain Science Production**: Always have labs producing science packs
2. **Plan Ahead**: Research prerequisite technologies before advanced ones
3. **Balance Research**: Don't neglect production while researching
4. **Use Bonuses**: Research speed and mining productivity early for compounding benefits
5. **Defend Your Base**: Research military technologies to protect against threats

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Research a Technology?
- Check prerequisites in the technology description
- Ensure you have enough science packs
- Make sure no other technology is currently being researched

### Research Not Progressing?
- Verify labs are powered and have science packs
- Check that inserters are supplying science packs to labs
- Ensure you have enough labs for the required science pack production

### No Technologies Available?
- You may have researched everything currently available
- Check for new technologies that require recently completed prerequisites
